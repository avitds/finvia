Frequently Asked Questions (FY 2025-26)

Which is better: old or new tax regime?

The better regime depends on your total income and deductions. If you claim multiple deductions (80C, 80D, HRA, etc.), the old regime may result in lower tax.

Is TDS mandatory for salaried employees?

Yes, TDS is deducted monthly by your employer based on your income and tax regime choice.

Can I claim both 80C and 80D?

Yes, 80C covers investments (LIC, PPF), while 80D is for medical insurance. You can claim both if eligible.

Is standard deduction allowed under the new regime?

Yes, ₹75,000 standard deduction is allowed under the new tax regime from FY 2023-24 onwards.

What is Section 87A rebate?

If total income is up to ₹7 lakh (new regime) or ₹5 lakh (old regime), you are eligible for 100% tax rebate under Section 87A.
