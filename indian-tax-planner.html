Plan Your Taxes Effectively

Tax planning helps you legally reduce your tax liability using deductions and exemptions available under the Income Tax Act. For FY 2025-26, you can choose between the Old vs New Tax Regime. Use our TDS Calculator to estimate your tax.

Key Tax-Saving Sections to Explore

  • Section 80C – Investments in LIC, PPF, ELSS, etc. (Up to ₹1.5 lakh)
  • Section 80D – Health insurance premium deduction
  • Section 80G – Donations to approved charitable institutions
  • Section 87A – Rebate for incomes under ₹7 lakh (new regime)
  • HRA – Claim exemption on house rent paid (if applicable)
  • EPF/NPS Contributions – Reduce taxable income with retirement savings

Tax Planning Tips

  • Always calculate using both old and new regime to choose the better one
  • Invest early in the financial year for better returns and less tax stress
  • Maintain receipts of all deductions like insurance, donations, rent, etc.
  • Use online tools like TheFinvia TDS Calculator for instant insights
