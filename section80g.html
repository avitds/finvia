What is Section 80G?

Section 80G of the Income Tax Act allows you to claim tax deductions for donations made to certain relief funds and charitable institutions. This deduction can be 50% or 100% of the donation amount depending on the organization.

Eligible Donations

  • Prime Minister's National Relief Fund – 100% deduction without limit
  • National Defence Fund – 100% deduction
  • Approved NGOs – 50% or 100% deduction with/without qualifying limit
  • Donations to notified temples/trusts with 80G approval

Important Points

  • Cash donations above ₹2,000 are not eligible (must be via cheque/bank transfer)
  • Keep donation receipt with PAN and 80G certificate of the organization
  • Corporates, firms, and HUFs can also claim this deduction
