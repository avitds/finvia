What is Section 80C?

Section 80C of the Income Tax Act allows individuals and Hindu Undivided Families (HUFs) to reduce taxable income by making eligible tax-saving investments or incurring qualified expenses. The maximum deduction under this section is ₹1,50,000 in a financial year.

Eligible Investments & Expenses under Section 80C

  • Life Insurance Premium (LIC)
  • Public Provident Fund (PPF)
  • Employees’ Provident Fund (EPF)
  • National Savings Certificate (NSC)
  • 5-Year Fixed Deposit with Banks
  • Tax Saving Fixed Deposits
  • Equity Linked Saving Scheme (ELSS)
  • Senior Citizens Savings Scheme (SCSS)
  • Post Office Time Deposit (5 years)
  • Home loan principal repayment
  • Tuition fees for children (max 2)
  • Sukanya Samriddhi Yojana
  • Unit Linked Insurance Plans (ULIP)
  • National Pension Scheme (NPS – Tier 1)
