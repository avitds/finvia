What is Section 80G?

Section 80G of the Income Tax Act allows you to claim deductions for donations made to specified funds, trusts, and charitable institutions. The deduction can be either 100% or 50% depending on the institution and is available only if donations are made via cheque, draft, or digital mode.

Types of Deduction under 80G

Category Deduction Qualifying Limit
PM National Relief Fund, CM Relief Fund 100% No limit
National Children’s Fund, Clean Ganga Fund 100% Without limit
Trusts like CRY, HelpAge India 50% Subject to 10% of Gross Total Income
Donations to Local Authorities (for family planning, etc.) 100% Subject to 10% of Gross Total Income

How to Claim 80G Deduction?

  • Donation should be made in a mode other than cash (cash allowed only up to ₹2,000)
  • Obtain a valid 80G receipt with PAN and registration number of the trust
  • Report donation in your ITR form under Chapter VI-A deductions

You can save significant tax and support meaningful causes at the same time. For salaried individuals and business owners alike, this deduction plays a vital role in tax planning.
