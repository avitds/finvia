Smart Tax Planning in India (2025-26)

Tax planning helps you legally reduce your tax liability through smart investments and by claiming eligible deductions and exemptions. Whether you're a salaried individual or self-employed, strategic planning ensures you don’t pay more tax than required.

Key Tax Saving Options

  • Section 80C: PPF, EPF, ELSS, LIC premium, tax-saving FD (limit: ₹1.5 lakh)
  • Section 80D: Health insurance premium for self/family (limit: ₹25,000 – ₹1,00,000)
  • HRA: House Rent Allowance deduction for salaried individuals
  • Standard Deduction: ₹50,000 (Old) and ₹75,000 (New) for salaried/pensioners
  • 80G: Donations to charities and relief funds
  • Section 87A: Tax rebate up to ₹25,000 for low-income taxpayers
