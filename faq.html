TDS or Tax Deducted at Source is the income tax deducted from your salary or payments before they are credited to you. It ensures timely collection of taxes.

Employers, companies, and organizations making specified payments such as salary, rent, professional fees, and interest are liable to deduct TDS as per the Income Tax Act.

You can view your TDS deductions through Form 26AS available on the Income Tax e-filing website using your PAN login.

If excess TDS has been deducted, you can claim a refund while filing your income tax return. The IT department will process and refund the amount if eligible.

TDS on salary is calculated based on applicable income tax slabs after allowing deductions like 80C, 80D, HRA, and standard deduction.
