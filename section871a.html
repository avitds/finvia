What is Section 87A?

Section 87A of the Income Tax Act offers a tax rebate to individuals whose net taxable income is within a certain limit. This helps reduce tax liability to zero if your total income is within the rebate threshold.

Section 87A Rebate for FY 2025-26

  • New Tax Regime: Rebate up to ₹25,000 if income is up to ₹7,00,000
  • Old Tax Regime: Rebate up to ₹12,500 if income is up to ₹5,00,000

This rebate is available only to resident individuals and not to HUFs or firms.

Who Can Claim?

  • Resident individuals below 60 years
  • Senior and super senior citizens can also claim, provided total income is within limits
  • No rebate is available for income above these thresholds

How to Apply Section 87A Rebate?

The rebate is automatically applied when you file your ITR (Income Tax Return). Use our TDS Calculator to check if you qualify.
