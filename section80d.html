What is Section 80D?

Section 80D of the Income Tax Act provides tax deductions for premiums paid towards medical insurance for yourself, your family, and parents. It also covers preventive health check-ups within the specified limit.

Who Can Claim?

  • Individual (self, spouse, and dependent children)
  • Parents (whether dependent or not)

Deduction Limits under Section 80D

  • ₹25,000: For self, spouse, and dependent children
  • ₹25,000: For parents (non-senior citizen)
  • ₹50,000: For parents (senior citizen)
  • ₹5,000: Preventive health check-up (within overall limits)

Taxpayers can claim up to ₹1,00,000 in total if both taxpayer and parents are senior citizens. Ensure premium is paid in any mode other than cash (except for preventive check-ups).
