What is Section 87A?

Section 87A provides a rebate on income tax for individual taxpayers whose total taxable income is within a certain limit. For FY 2025-26, you can get up to ₹25,000 rebate depending on your regime and income.

Eligibility for Section 87A in FY 2025-26

  • You must be a *resident individual* (not HUF or company).
  • Your total taxable income should be:
    • ≤ ₹5,00,000 under Old Regime
    • ≤ ₹7,00,000 under New Regime

Rebate Limit

Tax Regime Rebate Limit Income Limit
Old Tax Regime Up to ₹12,500 ₹5,00,000
New Tax Regime Up to ₹25,000 ₹7,00,000

Example

If your taxable income is ₹4,90,000 under the old regime, your calculated tax would be ₹12,000 – but Section 87A gives a rebate of ₹12,000, making your net tax zero.

Important Notes

  • Rebate is applied *after* total tax is calculated.
  • If your tax is ₹10,000, you get ₹10,000 rebate. If tax is ₹30,000, you get max ₹25,000 rebate (under new regime).
  • Standard deduction is still applicable before income is compared to threshold.
