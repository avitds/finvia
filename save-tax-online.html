Why Save Tax?

Tax planning is essential to optimize your income and secure your financial future. The Indian Income Tax Act offers multiple sections like 80C, 80D, HRA, and more to help individuals save tax legally.

Popular Tax Saving Sections

  • Section 80C: Investments in PPF, ELSS, LIC, NSC (Max ₹1.5 lakh)
  • Section 80D: Health insurance premiums (Up to ₹1 lakh)
  • HRA: House Rent Allowance based on salary and city
  • Standard Deduction: ₹50,000 (Old Regime), ₹75,000 (New Regime - FY 2025-26)
  • NPS: Additional ₹50,000 under Section 80CCD(1B)
