Common Questions About TDS & Tax Calculation

What is TDS in salary?

TDS (Tax Deducted at Source) is the income tax deducted by your employer from your salary every month and paid to the government on your behalf.

Which tax regime is better for FY 2025-26?

The best regime depends on your deductions. If you claim 80C, HRA, PF, and other deductions, the old regime might be better. Use TheFinvia Calculator to compare.

How do I calculate TDS for my income?

Use the TDS Calculator on TheFinvia. Input your income, age, deductions, and choose a tax regime. It instantly shows your monthly and annual TDS.

Is standard deduction available under the new regime?

Yes. Starting FY 2025–26, the standard deduction under the new regime is ₹75,000.
